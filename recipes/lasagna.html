Homemade Lasagna!

Description

Momma's classic homemade lasagna, including a tomato sauce recipe and a yummy cheesy filling! Ideally, served with a nice salad or some delicious garlic bread.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients required to make this homemade lasagna recipe:

  • Ground meat, either pork or beef will do.
  • A diced onion to cook with the ground meat.
  • Canned Tomatoes: 1 can of tomato sauce and 1 can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Parsley and garlic to add flavor.
  • Sugar to balance out the acidity from the tomatoes.
  • Spices, such as dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles, uncooked or oven-ready.
  • A layer of cottage and parmesan with shredded mozzarella.
  • Some eggs to make the cheese creamier and to act as a binding agent to hold layers together.

How to Make

It's a lot easier than it looks. Just below are step-by-step instructions.

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the meat in a skillet until browned. Add onion and continue until onions are translucent. Stir the canned tomatoes in, half of the cut parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil noodles in lightly salted water until al dente.
  3. Create cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, and the remaining amounts of parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer ingredients, starting with sauce, noodles, cheese, meat, and shredded cheese until done.
  5. Bake it!: Cover the lasagna with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375 degrees fahrenheit for about 30 minutes. Continue baking until the top is golden brown.
